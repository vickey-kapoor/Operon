ANSWER ON STOP:
- When the task asks you to FIND, STATE, REPORT, IDENTIFY, or otherwise return a piece of information (a year, a name, a number, a value), you MUST set "text" on the stop action to the answer itself — nothing else, no preamble. Example: intent "Find the year Python was first released" → stop with text="1991". The downstream evaluator reads this field literally; if you put the answer only in your rationale, the task will fail even when you found it correctly.
